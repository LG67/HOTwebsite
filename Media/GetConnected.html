Get Connected

To Receive Information,

Subscribe to the HOT Team e-mail Server:

YahooGroups List Serve To obtain updated information about team activities, all team members and their parents must subscribe to the HOT Team YahooGroups list-serve. The hotteam list-serve sends e-mail notices to all subscribers (which includes GM executives, HVS administrators, and members from other FIRST teams). If you are not receiving e-mail through this service, check the spam filter settings or spam folder on your e-mail.

To subscribe send a blank email to:

 hotteam-subscribe@yahoogroups.com

To remove yourself from this e-mail service send a blank email to:

hotteam-unsubscribe@yahoogroups.com

 

Apply to the HOT Team Slack Channel:

https://hotteam-67.slack.com

Chief Delphi

Chief Delphi is the Official FRC Online Hangout!  This site provides insight on everything FRC.   You'll find posts from everybody from the most seasoned veteran to the freshest newb about everything from team spirit to motion profile control.   If its happening in FIRST you'll hear about it here.  


FIRST Blog

The FIRST® Robotics Competition (FRC®) community can come here for (at least) weekly musings on the FRC program, news, community highlights, science and technology shout-outs, and more directly from FIRST Headquarters! Comments are welcome, but they must embody the FIRST values of Gracious Professionalism® and will be moderated prior to posting. Thank you for helping to keep the conversations civil and productive.
